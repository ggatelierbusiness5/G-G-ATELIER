The inspiration

Morning mist.
Measured movement.

The Backwaters Polo begins with still water and moving palms: the gentle contradiction of Kerala at first light.

Rather than reproducing a tropical scene, the concept reduces palm forms into a soft, repeating movement. Mist sage drifts across an ivory ground, with small water-blue detail and a deep navy collar bringing definition at the face. The motif is intended to feel calm from a distance and reveal its rhythm as the wearer approaches.

An airy piqué construction and cool touch define the current material direction. The proposed athletic line stays clean through the shoulder and upper torso without becoming restrictive. Final fibre content, print method, measurements and colour standards remain subject to production confirmation.

Design notes

Soft colour.
Clear intention.

01

Ivory ground

A softer alternative to optical white, intended to sit easily beside stone, sand, navy and faded denim.

02

Mist-sage rhythm

Original palm movement reduced into a subtle all-over repeat instead of an obvious Hawaiian-style print.

03

Deep navy collar

A composed contrast that frames the face and gives the pale body a precise finish.

04

Small signature

The proposed G&G mark remains tiny so the palette, cloth and wearer lead the experience.
