The most reliable starting point is not a label—it is a polo in your wardrobe that already feels right.

Before you choose

How to measure
a favourite polo.

Lay the garment flat on a firm surface without stretching it. Use a flexible tape, record in centimetres and measure twice. Compare garment to garment; body measurements and label sizes are not interchangeable.

01

Shoulder

Measure in a straight line from the outer edge of one shoulder seam to the other. A clean shoulder point creates the frame for an athletic fit.

02

Half chest

Measure from armpit seam to armpit seam with the polo flat. Double this only if you need the full garment circumference for comparison.

03

Body length

Measure from the highest shoulder point beside the collar down to the hem. Consider whether you normally wear the polo tucked or untucked.

04

Sleeve and opening

Measure from shoulder seam to sleeve edge, then across the sleeve opening. The intended line should follow the upper arm without pinching.

Our intended silhouette

Athletic,
never restrictive.

A refined polo should acknowledge the body without outlining every contour.

The G&G Atelier direction is clean through the shoulder and upper chest, with considered ease at the waist and hem. Fabric structure changes how those dimensions feel, so a dense piqué and a smoother jersey may not wear identically even when their flat measurements are close.

  • ShoulderThe seam should sit near the natural shoulder edge, not fall far down the arm or pull inward.
  • ChestThe placket should lie flat. Horizontal pulling or gaping between buttons usually signals insufficient ease.
  • SleeveThe cuff should follow the arm while allowing comfortable movement and a relaxed bend at the elbow.
  • HemAn untucked polo should finish in balanced proportion to the torso, without appearing cropped or excessively long.
Pre-launch sizing notice

Final size specifications are being confirmed.

We have deliberately not published a numeric size chart before production measurements are final. Invented dimensions would create false precision. When the commercial range is ready, this page will show garment measurements by size, measurement tolerances and any style-specific fit notes.

For early fit guidance, email the shoulder, half-chest, length and sleeve measurements of a polo you already enjoy wearing. The atelier can use those details as a conversation starter, not as a guarantee of final fit.
